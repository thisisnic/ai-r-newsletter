Between Positron Assistant, Databot, ellmer, chatlas, and their offshoots, there’s been a lot of LLM-related news coming out of Posit in 2025. Many folks across the organization felt that it was hard to keep up with new developments in the space, both internally and in the wider world. With this in mind, my colleague Sara Altman and I started working on an internal newsletter in June; released every two weeks, we’d give the most concise possible synopsis of movements within the company as well as the news from the wider space that we were paying the closest attention to.
